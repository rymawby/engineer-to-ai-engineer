# ROI and Business Thinking

AI engineering work should connect technical choices to value, cost, adoption, and operational impact.

## Concepts To Know

- Calculating ROI
- Automation coverage
- Cost vs value
- Latency and operational trade-offs

## Revision Prompts

- What business outcome is the AI system meant to improve?
- How much of the workflow can actually be automated?
- What work still needs human review?
- How do model, retrieval, and infrastructure costs compare with the value created?
- When is lower latency worth higher cost?

## Interview Angle

The best technical system is not always the best business system. Senior AI engineers can explain cost, risk, speed, accuracy, and user value in the same conversation.
