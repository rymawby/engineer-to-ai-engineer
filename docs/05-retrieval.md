# Retrieval

Retrieval is the process of finding the most relevant information before generation, action, or decision-making.

## Concepts To Know

- What retrieval means
- Retrieval vs tools
- Top-k retrieval
- Hybrid search
- Permission-aware retrieval

## Revision Prompts

- What does retrieval add to an LLM system?
- How is retrieval different from calling a tool?
- What does top-k mean?
- Why combine keyword search and vector search?
- How should permissions affect retrieved context?

## Interview Angle

Retrieval is not just search quality. It also includes access control, ranking, freshness, filtering, and deciding what context is safe to put in front of the model.
